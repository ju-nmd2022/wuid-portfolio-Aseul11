MAGAZINE SPREADS
Modern Interior Design Style magazine explains an important aspects of the Modern Design style, why it is so popular nowadays and how this style is different from others.
First spread descrides Modern Design and its history, and differentiates it from Contemporary Design Style, which is quite similar from the first glance.
Second spread is about basics of every design style - colours, lines, and shapes.
Third spread describes unique furniture style, which is used in Modern Interior Design, and such details as floor, walls, and ceiling look, since it is a core of this design style.
Last but not list, fourth spread talks about decorations and details in design, which plays an essencial role in the atmosphere of Modern Interior Design Style.
